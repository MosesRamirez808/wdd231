NP Membership Benefits

Non-Profit membership provides organizations with free access to the Chamber and its networking opportunities. Members can connect with local businesses, community leaders, and other non-profits while gaining visibility through participation in chamber-sponsored events and initiatives.

This membership level supports collaboration and outreach by offering networking events and inclusion in the Chamber community, helping non-profit organizations strengthen partnerships and expand their impact without financial barriers.

×

Bronze Benefits

Bronze membership offers essential exposure through a Chamber directory listing and access to networking events. Members receive discounted entry to select events, making it easier to build professional relationships and stay engaged with the local business community.

This level is ideal for organizations seeking foundational marketing support, including basic advertising opportunities that help increase awareness and establish a local presence.

×

Silver Benefits

Silver membership expands growth opportunities by providing access to professional training sessions and educational workshops designed to strengthen business operations and leadership skills.

Members also receive spotlight advertising and enhanced promotional opportunities, along with event sponsorship options that increase visibility and position the organization as an active community partner.

×

Gold Benefits

Gold membership delivers premium exposure through homepage spotlights and high-visibility advertising placements across Chamber platforms. Members benefit from maximum brand recognition and priority promotional opportunities.

This level includes access to exclusive VIP events and sponsorship opportunities, allowing organizations to take a leadership role in Chamber initiatives and community development efforts.
